The idea

One pool. A spectrum of holders.

The contract is the ERC-20, the owner of a single v4 liquidity position, and the fee router — all at once. Ownership of the token is ownership of the liquidity.

◈

Hold a facet

Each whole TEST mints one facet NFT — a DN404-style mirror of your balance — worth a slice of the same pool. Sell and it burns. Fewer facets, fatter slices.

≈

Fees stream in

Every swap pays fees straight to the position. They accrue to facet holders pro-rata — claimable on-chain, no third party, no lockups.

⬡

Supply is fixed

5,000 TEST, minted once, seeded entirely into the pool. No mint switch, no team bag dripping out. The float is the pool.

What makes it different

Three upgrades on the engine.

The same "holding is LPing" core, with economics rebuilt to reward conviction and grow the pool itself.

A

Loyalty weighting

Fees are split by weight, not headcount. A facet's weight climbs the longer you hold it — up to 2× — and resets the moment you sell. Diamond hands earn double; mercenaries earn base.

  • 1.00× → 1.25× (7d) → 1.50× (30d) → 2.00× (90d)
  • Permissionless bumpLoyalty()
B

POL flywheel

A slice of every fee (default 15%) is compounded straight back into the position. Liquidity deepens over time → less slippage → more volume → more fees. The prism grows.

  • Anyone can call compoundPOL()
  • Protocol-owned, never withdrawable
C

Dynamic fee

A v4 dynamic-fee hook: a decaying anti-snipe ramp at launch, a sell premium so dumps pay the holders, and a volatility component — all hard-capped at 10%.

  • Computed in beforeSwap
  • No honeypot fee is ever reachable

Fair by construction

No rug. By the absence of code.

These aren't promises — they're things the contract simply cannot do. Read the source.

✓

Fixed supply

Minted once in the constructor. No mint function, no owner mint path.

✓

Liquidity is locked

Nothing removes a non-zero amount of liquidity or moves the LP out. Fees collect; POL only adds.

✓

Fee capped at 10%

The dynamic fee is hard-capped in code. Owner setters are bounded. No 100% trap exists.

✓

No pause, no blacklist

No transfer tax to the owner, no freeze. Owner power: one-time seed + bounded params.
